01. About Me

Hello! I'm Rohan! I started developing when I got my first computer when I was 5 years old, and since then technology has gripped me. My primary love lies in learning and creating, whether that means reading about new theories for quantum gravity or building my own applications.

I think one of life's biggest wonders is innovation. One of my dreams is to create a place where children can see new inventions and be inspired, but until I can do that, I try to spread what I know to others.

Journalism

News & Features Section Editor for PHS's The Tower.

As a section editor for The Tower, I colead the News and Features section. I assign stories, edit drafts, and lead student writers to create articles about important issues in my school community. I publish articles that range from investigative features to spotlights on new teachers and interesting programs.

I also write opinion pieces about current issues that affect students, ranging from the use of AI to grade standardized tests to the disappearance of third spaces.
